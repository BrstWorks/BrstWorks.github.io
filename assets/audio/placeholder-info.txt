# Placeholder Audio Files

This folder should contain your MP3 files. Currently, it has placeholder files that will be replaced with your actual music.

## Expected Files:
- electronic-dreams.mp3
- midnight-vibes.mp3
- urban-pulse.mp3
- chill-waves.mp3

## How to Add Your Music:
1. Place your MP3 files in this folder
2. Name them exactly as listed above, OR
3. Update the playlist in script.js with your custom filenames

## Note:
If files are missing, the player will show a helpful message instead of trying to load non-existent URLs.
